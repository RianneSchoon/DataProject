... the influence of health care on life expectancy

To the introduction!

Do you want to live forever?

Most people do! However, even if you avoid accidents and murderers, you still have to face disease, infections, and eventually wear and tear due to old age - to eternity. Nonetheless, we still try to prolong our lifes as much as possible. The success of this endeavor varies greatly.

Let us explore some supporting means of health care and their influence on life expectancy around the world!

The health care variables are: amount of doctors, nurses, and available hospital beds; life expectancy; and Gross Domestic Product. The map, line chart and scatterplot let you explore the distribution, relation, and development of these variables. Select data with the buttons and select the year with the slider. Hover over graphs and click countries, lines and dots to see additional information pop up and to update the other graphs!
